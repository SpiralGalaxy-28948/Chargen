A few notes.

    
This will likely have bugs.

    
This is adapted to a a particular version of the hostile setting and may not provide useful characters for other settings.

    
There is no homeworld selection, Use the flowchart in Roll20 and make a note.
    

    
Each time the popup appears it should tell you what the dice roll is for, you can accept the dice roll it's done or put your own in.

    
Note that you get all your service skills at level 0 as soon as you enlist, and that you can be promoted in the same year as commissioning.

    
    
Investigate has been added from the Hostile Book.

    
The science skills from Traveller/Cepheus have been kept in as I don't like the setting version of Scientist.

    
The science skill cascades like vehicle and combat skills.
